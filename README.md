# Pricing Optimization & Customer Segmentation

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer transaction data to develop pricing strategies and optimize revenue. It combines customer segmentation with pricing analytics to simulate real-world business scenarios.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Organizations need to:
- Understand how pricing impacts demand and revenue
- Identify high-value customers
- Optimize pricing strategies for profitability
- Segment customers for targeted decision-making

---

## 🧠 Approach

### 1. Data Processing
- Loaded transactional dataset
- Derived revenue and demand features

### 2. Feature Engineering
- Revenue = Total transaction value
- Demand = Quantity proxy
- Price = Revenue per unit
- Simulated price elasticity to model demand changes

### 3. Pricing Optimization
- Modeled how demand changes with price variations
- Calculated optimized revenue based on elasticity

### 4. Customer Segmentation
- Applied KMeans clustering
- Segmented customers based on:
  - Revenue
  - Price sensitivity
  - Demand patterns

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Pricing changes directly impact demand and revenue
- High-value customers contribute disproportionately to revenue
- Segmentation helps align pricing strategies with customer behavior
- Elasticity modeling provides better pricing decisions
